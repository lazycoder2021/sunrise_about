WELCOME TO SUNRISE HOSPITAL

Welcome to Sunrise Hospital, F-1 Kalindi Colony next to New Friends Colony in New Delhi. We are specialized in Minimally Invasive Surgery which has been accredited worldwide. It provides Minimally Invasive procedures in all branches of medicine like Laparoscopy, Thoracoscopy, Arthroscopy & Cystoscopy with some of the most experienced doctors. Therefore, we proudly speak of our care, expertise and experience.

It is 50-bedded hospital located in the heart of South Delhi which is just 30 minutes drive from Indira Gandhi International Airport.

OUR MANAGEMENT TEAM

DR. HAFEEZ RAHMAN

Chairman – Gynaecologist and Laparoscopic Surgeon

Our Chairperson and the driving force behind Sunrise Hospitals, Dr. Hafeez Rahman began his career focusing on Laparoscopic surgery in 1992. Today, he is one of the pioneers in the world for this medical procedure. He performed more than 1,00,000 Surgeries and more than 230 Workshops & Seminars including Live surgery to AAGL (LAS VEGAS).
